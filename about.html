ABOUT FURNISH.LTD

Furnish.ltd is an indigenous Furniture and Interior Design Company which commenced business in 2007, with its factory located in Iju and showrooms at Ilupeju and Lekki phase1, Lagos.

We can proudly say that almost all our furniture pieces are produced locally while we get our fabrics from Turkey. Creating mess free, stress free homes that you can really live in no matter who or what you live with is our ultimate goal.

We focus on your family, your style and best of all...your budget!

You think hiring an interior designer will cost too much? It won't!!! Whatever your budget, whatever your style, we solve your designing dilemmas. Full service residential and commercial interior design, we have a great team to give our clients the best service.

We recognize that success depends on delivering a consistently high standard of service and Winnyz Interiors has been able to sustain this through leverage on its reliable and efficient services via the network of our associate/affiliated companies and we are actively engaged in the following:

- Hotel Furniture

- Home & Office Furniture

- Kitchen & Wardrobes

- Ceiling, Wall, & Floor Finishes

- Window Treatment; blinds & curtains

- Renovation & Makeovers

- Reupholster of used sofas

- Interior Design & Space Planning

- Accessories & Accents; rugs, vases, lamps

- Office Partitioning

Let us transform your space from concept to completion...all within your budget!
